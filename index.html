SUSTAINABLE ENERGY IS DEVELOPMENT...

Energy efficiency and renewable energy are said to be the twin pillars of sustainable energy. Suatainable energy is said to be one of the major pillars of Human Development. At 18650 Renewables we are passionate about bringing sustainable energy to the 1.3 billion people who lack access globally and also help mitigate green house gas emissions in the process. With a special focus on the African Continent which has a young rapidly growing population and a rising demand for reliable energy.

RENEWABLE ENERGY

18650 Renewables develops on small- to mid-sized hybrid solar power solutions for commercial or residential customers. Through its renewable energy solutions 18650 Renewables is able to reduce energy costs, create a stable power supply and reduce pollution..

SECOND LIFE BATTERY STORAGE

18650 Renewables sets up dedicated collection networks for discarded laptop batteries which may still have 60% of capacity left in them. Fractions of laptop batteries are sorted, refined, tested and reused for other applications.
